Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I interpret the prediction lines?
    A: The prediction lines show past (yellow), current (green), and future (red) traffic levels. Longer lines indicate higher traffic volumes.
  • Q: What do the colors in the status panel mean?
    A: Green indicates normal or optimal conditions, yellow indicates caution or minor issues, and red indicates critical issues or high traffic levels.
  • Q: How often is the data updated?
    A: By default, data is updated every 5 seconds. You can adjust this in the Settings page.
